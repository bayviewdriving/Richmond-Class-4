Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get my license?

Most experienced Class 5 drivers are test-ready in just 2 sessions. We focus on correcting "civilian" habits immediately to meet commercial standards.

Do I need a medical exam?

Yes. After passing your Knowledge Test, ICBC will provide a medical form. A doctor must complete this before you can book your road test appointment.

Can I use my own car for training?

Yes! We can train you on your personal vehicle for the Pre-Trip portion so you are comfortable with the exact car you'll use for the road test and ride-sharing.

Is the Handbook really free?

Yes. It is a $50 value. It contains all the verbal cues and technical checklists the examiners expect, allowing you to study at your own pace between lessons.
